RRSP

RRSP (Registered Retirement Savings Plan) is a Canadian government-approved investment account designed to help individuals save for retirement. Contributions to an RRSP are tax-deductible, meaning they reduce your taxable income for the year, and the investments grow tax-free until withdrawal.

TFSA

(Tax-Free Savings Account) is a flexible, tax-advantaged savings account available to Canadian residents aged 18 and older. It allows individuals to save and invest money tax-free throughout their lifetime.This ideal for short-term savings, emergency funds, or long-term investments, providing flexibility and tax advantages.

RESP

(Registered Education Savings Plan) is a Canadian tax-free savings account to help parents fund post-secondary education. Contributions grow tax-free, with government grants like CESG and CLB available. It has lifetime contribution limit and with flexible usage for education Unused funds can be withdrawn to an RRSP.
